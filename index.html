Realtime Face Detection and Expression Classification

This tool will access your device's webcam to capture a video feed. That video will be fed through a face recognition algorithm (in this case, face-api.js) that attempts to detect any faces in the video. It provides a rating from 0 to 1 showing how sure the algorithm is that what it sees is a human face. In addition, it will attempt to classify the emotion on any face it finds.

All processing for this tool happens client-side, within your browser. This means that your video is not sent to or stored on our servers. We do not keep your image in any way.

Press to start capturing video. Press the button again to stop.
